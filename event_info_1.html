Escape Room

September 25, 2025

MPH 2, 3rd Floor, EN Building

Students sharpened their analytical skills as they took on the ACSS Escape Room Challenge! Up to four players tried to find, decode, and assemble clues that could help them escape the room. The theme centered around a robot deciphering its emotions to become human. Open to all COE College Students, the event was held on September 25, 2024 at MPH 2 EN Building.
