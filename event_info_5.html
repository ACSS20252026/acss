Langit, Lupa, ACSScape Room

March 3, 2025 to March 4, 2025

MPH 2, 3rd Floor, EN Building

UE Warriors attempted to crack their brains as they solved riddles, found clues, and raced against the clock to escape the room. Langit, Lupa, ACSScape Room took childhood games and took it to a whole new level! The event was open to all college students and was held at MPH 2 3rd Floor EN Building on March 3-4, 2025 from 9:30am to 5:00pm.
