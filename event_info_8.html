Tekken Rumble

September 24, 2024 11:00am to 4:30pm

EPE Laboratory 3 and 4

Dozens of players stepped into the arena for the ACSS x AEES Tekken Rumble on September 24 (Tuesday) at EPE Laboratory 3 and 4 - 11:00 am to 4:30 pm. Top fighters faced off against each other in an intense Tekken 8 Tournament. The event was open to all departments and participants may also bring their own controllers. The winner of the tournament received a grand prize of P1000.00.
