Lights, Camera, ACSSion

March 3, 2025 to March 6, 2025

Theme: Campus Vibes: A Week of Fun and Unity

On March 3, 2025, The Association of Computer Studies Students (ACSS) revived their online photograph contest with the new theme “Campus Vibes: A Week of Fun and Unity.” Submissions for the event were accepted for 3 days starting when the mechanics were uploaded on the ACSS Facebook Page. The photographs were judged on a specific criteria:

- 40% - Relevance to the theme/message - The Photo content is relevant and adheres to the guidelines of the competition
- 30% - Creativity - The photo is taken from a creative standpoint or images is displayed distinctively and artistically
- 20% - Technicality - Clear subject in photo, good positioning, framed correctly, and no distracting elements that could be avoided
- 10% - Impact - The photo leaves an impression and the image attracted attention and feelings
