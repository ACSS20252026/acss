Lights, Camera, ACSSion

November 13, 2023 to November 17, 2023

Theme: MANDIRIGMA NG SILANGAN

On November 13, 2023, The Association of Computer Studies Students (ACSS) launched an online photograph contest with the theme “MANDIRIGMA NG SILANGAN.” Submissions for the event were accepted for 5 days starting when the mechanics were uploaded to the ACSS Facebook Page. The photographs were judged on a specific criteria:

- 40% - Relevance to the theme/message - The photo content is relevant and adheres to the guidelines of the competition
- 30% - Creativity - The photo is taken from a creative stand point or images is dislayed distinctively and artistically
- 20% - Technicality - Clear subject in photo, good positioning, framed correctly, and no distracting elements that could be avoided
- 10% - Impact -The photo leaves an impression and the image attracted attention and feelings

The photo submissions were uploaded to the ACSS Facebook Page on November 22, 2023 to start the race for reactions and shares. Winners of the event were announced on December 9, 2023.
