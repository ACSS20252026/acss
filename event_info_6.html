A ConfeSSion for you

February 10, 2025 to February 14, 2025

Online

The Association of Computer Studies Students (ACSS) launched an online event for Valentines Day where students can anonymously confess to their crushes by submitting their message through Google Forms. The ACSS accepted submissions from February 10, 2025 to February 14, 2025. The messages were uploaded to the ACSS Facebook Page on the night of Valentines Day.
